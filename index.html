Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I transfer my data from the official app to the modded app??

A: Open your file explorer (if you are on android 10 or up you will need to use https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.speedsoftware.explorer), navigate to Android/data, and rename the com.bandainamcoent.dblegends_ww folder to com.bandainamcoent.dblegends_wx.


Q: I can't transfer my account data!!!

A: You can't transfer using google accounts, try linking to something else, or copying your account file located at Android/data/ com.bandainamcoent.dblegends_ww/ files/ ecd1bb8b626d380e93748523485ef051


Q: Can I use mods in pvp/coop?

A: You can instawin in coop and raid, aswell as complete all challenges. You can also speedhack before the game loads so the opponent disconnects, and use one hit ko for damage (this is safe). Using instawin in ranked repeatedly will reset your RP (you shouldn't be cheating in ranked anyways).
